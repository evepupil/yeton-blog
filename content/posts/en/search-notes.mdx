## Make every result useful

Show enough context to let readers decide before they open a result: title, matched excerpt, date and topic.

## Keep languages separate by default

A reader searching the English site usually wants English results. Language boundaries reduce noise and make ranking easier to understand.

### Preserve a deliberate escape hatch

Cross-language search can still be offered as an explicit option for readers who need the full archive.
